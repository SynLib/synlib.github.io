A library for synopses implemented at Eindhoven University of Technology

About SynLib

SynLib is a collection of implementations of synopses developed at Eindhoven University of Technology (TU/e).

The library consists of our implementations of existing synopses in literature, e.g. Count-Min sketch [1] and synopses developed at TU/e, e.g. ECM-sketch [2] & OmniSketch [3].

Co-funded by EU-horizon projects STELAR (101070122) & Smart-Data Lake (825041).
